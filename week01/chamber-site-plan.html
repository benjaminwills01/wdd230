The Kalk Bay Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The site name is The Kalk Bay Chamber of Commerce. It was chosen to represent the local chamber, focusing on promoting businesses and attracting tourists to the town.

Site Purpose

  • Promote local businesses and foster business-to-business networking.
  • Provide information about local attractions, events, and demographics to attract tourism and investment.

Scenarios

  • What events are happening in the area this month?
  • How can I contact the chamber’s leadership?
  • What are the demographics and economic data of the town?

Color Schema

The selected colors are:

  • Primary Color: #0b3948 (Dark Blue) – for background accents and headings.
  • Secondary Color: #416165 (Gold) – for navigation, buttons and highlights.

Typography

The chosen fonts are:

  • Heading Font: Roboto – for all headings.
  • Body Font: Open Sans – for general text content.
